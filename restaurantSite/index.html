About US

Our founder, Eric Thomas, had an idea to create a restaurant that was more than a place that served great food. Emilio wanted to give locals a place where they'd always feel at home. A place where they could authentic nicaraguan food at down-to-earth prices, and where they'd be treated right by people who had a passion to serve.

The culture at the World Cusine Corner was instilled by Eric Thomas long before a mission statement was ever written down on paper and is still evident today. At the Nicaraguan Corner, guests, team members and community are all treated with respect.
